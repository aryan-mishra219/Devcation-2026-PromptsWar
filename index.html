Journey

Event Timeline

From registration to the grand finale — here's your roadmap.

Phase 1

Registration

Open to all. Form your team and sign up.

Registrations are open for all undergraduate and postgraduate students. Form a team of 2–4 members, submit your idea, and get ready for an unforgettable experience. Early-bird registrations come with exclusive perks!

Phase 2

Hack 'N' Solve Submission

48-hour online hacking sprint.

Teams will have 48 hours to build and submit their projects. You'll have access to mentor office hours, cloud credits, and API resources. Submit your project on the official portal with a demo video and documentation.

Phase 3

Mentorship Round

1-on-1 sessions with industry experts.

Top-selected teams get exclusive mentorship sessions with Google Developer Experts, startup founders, and senior engineers. Refine your solution, get feedback, and polish your pitch for the finals.

Grand Finale

Finale at IIT Delhi

Present on stage. Win big.

The top teams will be invited to IIT Delhi for an in-person grand finale. Present your project to a distinguished panel of judges, network with industry leaders, and compete for exciting prizes, goodies, and internship opportunities!
